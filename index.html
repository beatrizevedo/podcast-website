About the podcast

‘Vamos tratar de vida’ means ‘Let’s talk about life’. It’s a portuguese podcast, created by Mariana, Pedro and, of course, their daughter Alice. During more than an hour they talk about their lives, sports, parenthood questions, finance, work and daily struggles and goals. Mariana and Pedro were already known on Instagram but in their podcast they share much more than that, with lots of humor and love.
